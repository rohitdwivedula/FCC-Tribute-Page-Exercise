Mahatma Gandhi

The Freedom Fighter

Here's a timeline of Mahatma's lifetime:
  • 1869: Born in Porbandar, Gujarat.
  • 1893: Arrives in South Africa at the age of 24. Spent 21 years here and fought against apartheid in South Africa
  • 1915: Gandhi returns to India
  • 1918: Nonviolent Champaran and Kheda satyagraha forces British to gove concessions
  • 1920: Non-cooperation movement launched
  • 1921: Non-cooperation movement called off at its peak as Gandhi feared movement was turning violent
  • 1930: Through the Dandi Satyagraha Gandhi renevives civil disobedience movement
  • 1933: The Harijan movement to fight for the right of dalits
  • 1934: Gandhi resigns from the Indian National Congress
  • 1942: The quit India movement is launched, demanding for complete independence
  • 1947: India granted independence. Mahatma tries to control the Hindu-Muslim riots
  • 1948: Gandhi assassinated on 30th January, 1948. His last words were "Hey ram" which translates to "Oh God"

In my life I have always looked to Gandhi as an inspiration because he embodies the kind of transformational change that can be made when ordinary people come together to do extraordinary things.

President of the US Barack Obama
